A city with a rich history, world-famous university traditions and «visiting cards», does not stop attracting foreign students. A two-minute walk to the metro opens up the city, where the old streets and lush greenery are unusually unique and reveal beautiful pavement, cobblestone and unforgettable tourist attractions.
