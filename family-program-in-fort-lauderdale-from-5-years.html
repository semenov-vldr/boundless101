The TALK Family Package provides a stimulating learning environment for families to learn English while vacationing in the USA. The program is offered during winter and summer.

Parents and children 13 years of age will join the part-time General English course, which features interactive classroom technology.

Children aged 5 to 12 will join the Kids English course, which features activities and games to optimize learning:

  • Teaching is based on weekly learning themes supplemented with fun activities such as games, stories and songs.
  • Themes may include “Seasons & Weather”, “Occupations & Hobbies”, “Your Family Tree”, “Science & Space”, etc.
  • One special weekly activity such as a field trip, scavenger hunt, animal show, making & eating ice-cream sundaes, etc.
  • Graduation ceremony. Parents are invited!
  • Classrooms are equipped with age-appropriate materials, toys and games.

Schedule: Monday – Thursday from 9:00 to 13:10. Lessons for kids and adults are held at the same time.
